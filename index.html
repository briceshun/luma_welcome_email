Boris Marinov
Hello! I'm Boris, a recent AI graduate who is thrilled to be starting in Luma! I'm originally Bulgarian, grew up in Christchurch, completed both my degrees at the University of Groningen in the Netherlands and have now returned back to this side of the world. I really enjoy discovering new things in data sets and am curious about all aspects of Data Science, AI and Machine Learning. In particular, I find the field of Natural Language Processing fascinating and really exciting. When I'm not staring at my computer, I try to catch up with painting projects, improve my yoga poses and skating or dig for new music. Some of the things I value are curiosity, hard work, awareness for the environment and good coffee! Can't wait to meet everyone on the team and start diving into some projects together!
